Los Angeles Lakers

Los Angeles Lakers. Not only the most popular NBA franchise, but also the most successful with 17 titles. They are the current champions, thanks to coach Frank Vogel, power forward Anthony Davis, and my personal favourite player of all-time: LeBron James. Legends like Kobe Bryant, Kareem Abdul-Jabaar, and Shaquille O'Neal have all played for the franchise.

Vitoria de Setubal

Even though football is not a sport I watch too often, this club will always have a special place in my heart, as it is my hometown club. One of the most successful clubs in Portuguese football, after the 3 major teams. Currently relegated to the 3rd division of professional football, mostly due to internal problems relating to debt and failure to pay players.
