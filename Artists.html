Kendrick Lamar

Hailing from Compton, California, Lamar is a rapper, songwriter, and producer whose growth has been astronomic since his studio debut in 2011. With classic albums such as "good kid, m.A.A.d city" and "To Pimp A Butterfly", Kendrick has always been one of the top artists in the hip-hop scene, it was only recently that he began to enter mainstream media with his album "DAMN.". You don't need to like rap to enjoy his music, as he has a lot of influences from jazz, blues, and other kinds of genres in his discography.

Frank Ocean

Originally from Long Beach, California, Ocean is a singer/songwriter who started his career as a ghostwriter for artists like Justin Bieber and John Legend. He has gained popularity in the pop world for his affectionate and intimate lyrics, taking inspiration from R&B and traditional pop music. His most recent album "Blonde" catapulted him to fame, with help from songs like "Nights", "Godspeed", and "Self Control".
