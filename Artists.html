Kendrick Lamar

Hailing from Compton, California, Lamar is a rapper, songwriter, and producer whose growth has been astronomic since his studio debut in 2011. With classic albums such as "good kid, m.A.A.d city" and "To Pimp A Butterfly", Kendrick has always been one of the top artists in the hip-hop scene, it was only recently that he began to enter mainstream media with his album "DAMN.". You don't need to like rap to enjoy his music, as he has a lot of influences from jazz, blues, and many more.

Frank Ocean

Originally from Long Beach, California, Ocean is a singer/songwriter who started his career as a ghostwriter for artists like Justin Bieber and John Legend. He has gained popularity in the pop world for his affectionate and intimate lyrics, taking inspiration from R&B and traditional pop music. His most recent album "Blonde" catapulted him to fame, with help from songs like "Nights", "Godspeed", and "Self Control".

Rosalia

Spanish act Rosalia, born in Barcelona, Spain, is a singer-songwriter initially known for her contemporary interpretations of flamenco music. Rosalia has been receiving huge international attention in the last year, due to collaborations with artists like Billie Eilish, The Weeknd, and Travis Scott. The singer's last project is a concept album, where every song talks about a specific topic, like: fate, jealousy, closure, and liturgy.

Slow J

Joao Batista Coelho, mostly known as Slow J, is a Portuguese rapper, record producer, and singer-songwriter. He is one of the biggest artists in Portuguese hip-hop, due to his magnificent artistic skills. Originally from my hometown of Setubal, Joao also went on to study in the UK, where he pursued sound engineering. Slow J is mostly known for his greater-than-life songs like "Serenata" (Serenade) and Lagrimas (Tears), as well as depicting societal issues in his songs.

Harry Styles

England native Harry Styles first gained popularity during his time as a One Direction singer, after participating in the show X Factor. Known mostly for his pop-ish style during his early 20s, Harry is now making a beautiful transition into a more indie-esque style, with songs like "Fine Line" and "Golden", reminiscent of acts such as Novo Amor and Bon Iver.

Arctic Monkeys

Formed in Sheffield in 2002, Arctic Monkeys are a rock band lead by vocalist Alex Turner. Surprisingly a band that has been popular since their very first album, which is one of the most critically acclaimed projects of the last 20 years in the UK, and the fastest selling debut album in UK chart history, selling 363,735 copies in the first week. The band began getting major international recognition after their 5th studio album, "AM".
